Borrowing Sports Equipment

Welcome! To borrow school sports equipment, you need to log in with your email and password. Once verified, you can set a timer for how long you intend to use the equipment.

After the timer expires, a reminder will be sent to your email. If you don't return the equipment after three reminders, you will be asked to see the dean.

Please make sure to return the equipment on time to avoid any issues. Click "Next" to proceed.
